# Business Requirements

## Project Overview

The Retail Analytics Platform is an end-to-end Analytics Engineering project designed to demonstrate how raw retail data can be transformed into trusted, analytics-ready datasets for business reporting.

This project simulates a real-world retail company that collects data from multiple operational systems, including orders, customers, products, inventory, marketing campaigns, and product returns.

---

## Business Problem

Retail organizations often experience inconsistent reporting because different departments calculate key business metrics differently.

Common challenges include:

- Inconsistent revenue reporting
- Duplicate business metrics
- Poor data quality
- Missing customer information
- Manual spreadsheet reporting
- Slow reporting processes

These issues reduce confidence in business reports and make decision-making more difficult.

---

## Project Objectives

The goal of this project is to build a modern analytics platform that:

- Creates a single source of truth
- Standardizes business metrics
- Improves data quality
- Automates data transformations
- Supports executive reporting
- Enables scalable analytics

---

## Stakeholders

This platform supports:

- Executive Leadership
- Sales Team
- Marketing Team
- Finance Department
- Operations Team

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful when:

- Business metrics are standardized
- Data quality issues are detected automatically
- Reports are generated from trusted datasets
- Dashboard metrics remain consistent across departments

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Gross Profit
- Average Order Value
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Return Rate
- Marketing ROI
- Inventory Turnover
