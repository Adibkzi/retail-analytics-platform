# Metric Definitions

## Overview

This document defines the core business metrics used in the Retail Analytics Platform.

Standardized metric definitions ensure that all departments calculate KPIs consistently.

---

## Total Revenue

**Definition:**  
Total sales generated before returns and refunds.

**Why it matters:**  
Measures overall business performance.

---

## Gross Profit

**Definition:**  
Revenue minus cost of goods sold.

**Why it matters:**  
Shows how much profit remains after product costs.

---

## Net Profit

**Definition:**  
Gross profit minus operating expenses.

**Why it matters:**  
Measures overall profitability.

---

## Average Order Value (AOV)

**Definition:**  
Total revenue divided by total number of orders.

**Why it matters:**  
Shows how much customers spend per order on average.

---

## Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

**Definition:**  
Estimated total revenue generated by a customer over their relationship with the business.

**Why it matters:**  
Helps identify valuable customer segments.

---

## Return Rate

**Definition:**  
Returned orders divided by total orders.

**Why it matters:**  
Tracks product quality, customer satisfaction, and operational issues.

---

## Marketing ROI

**Definition:**  
Revenue generated from marketing campaigns divided by campaign cost.

**Why it matters:**  
Measures marketing effectiveness.

---

## Inventory Turnover

**Definition:**  
Cost of goods sold divided by average inventory.

**Why it matters:**  
Shows how efficiently inventory is being sold and replaced.
